Using snowflake data cloud

Our Sustainability pack ensures blending of both OT and IT data from SCADA systems,edge devices and ERP systems.It provides an easily deployable features and machine learning algorithms runs over historical data to recommend and implement optimal control settings on industrial asset helping utilites ensure high asset uptime, increase overall equipment effectiveness(OEE) and maximise renewable energy production.

Using anomaly detection techniques

It is made possible to monitor equipment sensors and detect any deviations from normal operating conditions. This allows for the early detection of potential equipment failures,minimizing downtime and production losses.

Reduce adverse environmental and social effects

By ensuring enhanced agility and flexibility, minimized carbon emissions, minimized energy usage and improved safety and security
